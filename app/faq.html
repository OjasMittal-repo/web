Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Newsletter Hub

Account & Subscription

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. You can register using your email address or through social media accounts like Google or Facebook. No credit card is required for free accounts.

Can I change my email preferences?

Yes! Go to your Account Settings > Email Preferences. You can choose how often you receive notifications and which types of newsletters we recommend.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

Visit your Account Settings > Subscriptions. Click "Manage" next to your premium subscription and follow the prompts to cancel. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Newsletters

How do I subscribe to newsletters?

Browse our categories or use the search function. When you find a newsletter you like, click the "Subscribe" button. You can manage all subscriptions from your dashboard.

Why am I not receiving my newsletters?

First check your spam/junk folder. If the newsletter isn't there, try these steps:
1. Verify your email in Account Settings
2. Ensure you haven't unsubscribed
3. Whitelist @newsletterhub.com in your email client

Can I suggest a newsletter to be added?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions. Email us at ojasmittal08@gmail.com with the newsletter details. Our curation team reviews all submissions weekly.

Technical Issues

The website isn't loading properly

Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Update your browser to the latest version
3. Try accessing from a different browser or device
If issues persist, contact our support team.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires in 24 hours for security.
